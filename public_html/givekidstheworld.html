The story of Give Kids The World begins with a little girl with a wish and the desire of one man to make that wish come true.

Amy had leukemia and one wish - to visit the theme parks in Orlando. To facilitate Amy's wish, the request of a complimentary stay was made to a respected hotelier. As he had done many times before, the hotelier gladly obliged and Amy's wish was that much closer to being realized. Sadly, the remainder of Amy's travel plans took too long to arrange and her wish was never granted; Amy had passed away. Time simply ran out.

Give Kids The World Village is a 70 acre, non –profit  “story-book” resort, located near Central Florida ‘s most beloved attractions, where children with life-threatening illnesses and their families are treated  to weeklong , cost- free fantasy vacations.

Members of the Barbados Association of Central Florida and Tampa Bay shared their time and talents at Give Kids the World Adopt- A- Villa Beautification program on June 22, 2013. Our project included sprucing up, clean up, painting and landscaping these buildings that our special families call home while on their dream vacation.

While sharing our time and talents, it was an afternoon of fun and team building like no other and we left satisfied knowing that we have truly made a difference in the life of a special child.

In addition, we are pleased to announce that the Barbados Association of Central Florida & Tampa Bay has adopted 2 villas at Give Kids the World Village. We will welcome our first "Wish Child " and family from Precious Touch Foundation Inc. of Barbados on July 21, 2013.

Precious Touch Foundation Inc. is a Wish Granting Organization in Barbados catering to the wishes of children with life threatening medical conditions from age 2 - 16 years old.
