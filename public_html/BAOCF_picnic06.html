BAOCF ANNUAL PICNIC 2006
The Barbados Association of Central Florida invites you to their Annual Picnic on Saturday September 16th, 2006 in Barnett Park Pavillion #4. Ticket prices are $10 for adults and $5.00 for children 10 years and under. There will be live music, games, food and drinks for all. The festivities will last from 12pm to 6pm. Be there to enjoy good times with fellow Bajans and west indians. Look below for directions to Barnett Park. Hope to see you there.
