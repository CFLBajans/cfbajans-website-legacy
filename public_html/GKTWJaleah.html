GKTW, CBAOCF &BAOTB Welcome Jaleah Hoyte "Wish Child"

 

 
Members of the Barbados Associations of Central Florida and Tampa Bay welcomed Jaleah Hoyte "Wish Child" of Precious Touch Foundation Inc., Barbados to Give Kids the World Village on April 11th, 2015. A warm reception was held at the Villa.   Jaleah returns to Barbados April 18th. Jaleah was presented with a laptop by the Coalition for the Barbados Association of Central Florida and Tampa Bay.
