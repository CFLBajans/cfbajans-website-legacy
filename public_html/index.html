BARBADOS ASSOCIATION OF CENTRAL FLORIDA
PRESIDENT'S MESSAGE

On behalf of the entire Board of Directors, I would like to welcome you to the Coalition for the Barbados Association of Central Florida. Our association has just celebrated its 25th birthday and currently has a membership affiliation of approximately 150 individuals. We have as one of our major initiatives a pledge to provide Barbados with charitable assistance that will help to ensure that a decent standard of living in Barbados continues, especially for the elderly.  The financial and physical contributions to the local community and to our home country of Barbados have had a great impact but I believe we still have a long way to go.

Over the past 25 years, we have made significant contributions to the local Central Florida community and to charities, individuals and schools in Barbados.  Our adoption and support of the Barbados St. Lucy’s District Hospital is one of the institutions that we believe will help to improve the standard of living for the elderly in Barbados.  We intend to supply this facility and others alike with needed physical plant items, clothing and medical supplies.  These items come at a high cost and this is where we need your support through donations, supporting our events, becoming a member of the CBAOCF, or providing leads to help us source items at reduced cost.  Every bit of help counts. We will continue to offer our annual scholarship to a worthy student of Barbadian heritage who resides in our local community. 

We have a very active calendar of events for 2016. These planned activities are all intended to raise funds for the association and to help us become more visible in the Central Florida community.  Please make an effort to attend one or all of our monthly meetings which are held at St. Mary’s of the Angels Church, 6316 Matchett Road, Orlando, Florida.  Monthly meetings are held on the 3rd Saturday of each month and begin at 6:30 pm. Hope to see you there.

Sincerely,
Dr. Dale Husbands
President, CBAOCF

Mission Statement

The mission of the Coalition for the Barbados Association of Central Florida is to encourage networking and promote kinship among Barbadians living in Central Florida. We are an association that maintains high standards and promote awareness of its endeavors to contribute to the development of Central Florida.  We intend to open and maintain avenues of communication between Barbadians in Central Florida and their homeland; and to foster educational, cultural and business operations to further the joint interests of Barbadians in both regions.

Future plans include ultimate ownership of real property in Central Florida for the benefit of resident and visiting Barbadians and to continue raising funds to provide scholarships for students pursuing a degree at recognized higher education institution.  In addition, we will continue our efforts to provide assistance and medical care for the needy, to support local and international relief efforts and finally to provide food, clothing and assistance at local homeless shelters.
