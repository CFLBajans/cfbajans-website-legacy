CBAOCF & Precious Touch Foundation Inc work together to help Children of Queen Elizabeth Hospital in Barbados

THREE ORGANIZATIONS collaborated recently to shower the children of the Queen Elizabeth Hospital, Bridgetown with gifts. The Give Kids The World Village, The Coalition for the Barbados Association of Central Florida and The Precious Touch Foundation Inc. pooled their resources to present Ward C8 with a DVD player, T-shirts, DVDs,books and a board game. Sonia Moore, a volunteer co-ordinator with both the Village and the association, said she liaised with Precious Touch a few years ago and they had been firm partners
ever since.
“When Precious Touch sends children to Disney World in Florida, the Village arranges the accommodation, and the association had adapted two villas where the family stays. I co-ordinate all the
arrangements and activities, but I don’t mind, it’s my country, so I do it for Barbados and the kids,” she said. Here, Moore (left) presenting a gift to senior nursing officer Marita Harris
as (from left) president of Precious Touch, Adorial Maxwell-Hazel,
vice-president St Auban Callender and QEH nursing officer Angela Lovell look on. (CA) (Picture by Lennox Devonish.)

Let's give Sonia Moore a big shout out and thank you for all she does for our country and community.
